Arena Event: February 2020 Rose Cup

Get ready for a new challenge beginning on February 1st! With it comes a new chance to prove your skill and increase your global and local player ranking! The Rose Cup - designed to display the dazzling strength of a Pokémon and its trainer - follows the same basic rules as all Silph Arena global cups, but this month it comes with a special twist!

Arena Event: January 2020 Fusion Cup

Show your skills and increase your global and local player rankingin a brand new challenge starting January 1st! The Fusion Cup - where trainers will prove their mastery of dual-type Pokémon - follows the same basic rules as all Silph Arena ranked tournaments, but with a special twist! With a meta sure to save you on stardust, it'll be a great opener for a new year of PvP.

Arena Event: December 2019 Timeless Cup

The Silph Arena’s third global cup of Season 2 begins on December 1st! With it comes a new chance to prove your skill and increase your global and local player ranking! The Timeless Cup - a flash back to the beginning of your Pokémon journey - follows the same basic rules as all Silph Arena global cups, but this month it comes with a special twist!

Arena Event: November 2019 Ferocious Cup

An all-new test of your skills lays before you: The Ferocious Cup! Two and Four-legged beasts of the wild will clash in an epic land battle! Prepare to Face Pokemon never seen at the top of the food chain before and prove your Pokémon mastery in the second global Cup of the competitive PvP Season. Use this link for the eligible species list as well as resources to help you prepare for battle!

Arena Event: October 2019 Sinister Cup

Announcing: the Sinister Cup! Starting October 1st, enter the shadows with your strongest Ghost, Psychic, Steel, & Fighting types! Note: you'll leave your Dark-types, Mythicals, Skarmory, and Hypno on the bench this month! Study up & we'll see you in the Arena for the first challenge of the Season!

Arena Asset: Season Two Roadmap

The time has come to show your strength in battle! The second season of The Silph Arena begins in just under a month, and with it comes new challenges, unique metas, and a chance to write yourself into local, national, and global legend!

Arena Event: August 2019 Mirror Cup

The eighth official Arena Cup, the Mirror Cup, runs from August 1, 2019 through August 31. For the first time in the Arena, the Mirror Cup provides tournament organizers a chance to select and host any Arena Cup from Season 1 (plus the Season 1 Regional Invitational rules).

Arena Event: July 2019 Jungle Cup

The seventh official Arena Cup, the Jungle Cup runs from July 1, 2019 through July 31 and features a restricted type-set: Electric, Bug, Grass, and Normal - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp) - along with species restrictions banning Tropius and the Burmy family.

Arena Event: June 2019 Rainbow Cup

The sixth official Arena Cup, the Rainbow Cup runs from May 1, 2019 through May 31 and features a restricted type-set: Psychic, Dark, and Fighting - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp) - along with species restrictions banning legendaries, mythicals, Sableye and Medicham.

Arena Event: Season 1 Regional Invitationals

Season 1's Regional Invitationals run from May 10, 2019 through May 26 and feature a broad type-set: any species eligible for Boulder, Twilight, Tempest, or Kingdom Cups - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp) - for qualifying invitees only.

Arena Event: May 2019 Nightmare Cup

The fifth official Arena Cup, the Nightmare Cup runs from May 1, 2019 through May 31 and features a restricted type-set: Psychic, Dark, and Fighting - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp) - along with species restrictions banning legendaries, mythicals, Sableye and Medicham.

Arena Event: April 2019 Kingdom Cup

The fourth official Arena Cup, the Kingdom Cup runs from April 1, 2019 through April 30 and features a restricted type-set: Fire, Ice, Dragon, and Steel - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp).

Arena Event: March 2019 Tempest Cup

The third official Arena Cup, the Tempest Cup runs from March 1, 2019 through March 31 and features a restricted type-set: Electric, Ice, Flying, and Ground - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp).

Arena Event: February 2019 Twilight Cup

The second official Arena Cup, the Twilight Cup runs from February 1, 2019 through February 28 and features a restricted type-set: Dark, Fairy, Poison, and Ghost - fought in the Great League (max 1500cp).

Arena Event: January 2019 Boulder Cup

The first official Arena Cup, the Boulder Cup runs from January 6, 2019 through January 31 and features a restricted type-set: Rock, Ground, Fighting, and Steel - fought in the Great League (under 1500cp).

Arena Asset: Arena 'Crest' Logo Graphic

The Arena 'Crest' logo is the primary mark of the Arena. You may feel free to add the Arena 'Crest' to your graphics to denote that your tournaments will be ranked or facilitated via Silph.gg and count in the Arena.
